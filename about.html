About Us

The Story Behind the Stories

Garden Route Living began the way most good things do around here: over a glass of wine, watching the sun set over the lagoon, wondering why nobody was telling these stories.

The Garden Route has always attracted writers, artists, and dreamers. But somewhere between the tourist brochures and the estate agent listings, the real stories were getting lost. The third-generation fisherman in Knysna. The winemaker who gave up corporate life to plant vines in Harkerville. The restaurant where the chef still goes out on his own boat every morning.

We started Garden Route Living in 2026 to tell those stories. Not the generic "top 10 things to do" content that could be written by anyone, anywhere. The stories that can only come from being here, knowing people, understanding what makes this stretch of coastline unlike anywhere else in the world.

We are a small team of writers, photographers, and locals who believe that great storytelling starts with genuine curiosity and deep respect for the people and places we cover.

What We Believe

Local First

We live here. We write about what we know and love.

Quality Over Quantity

Fewer stories, told well. No clickbait, no filler.

Independent Always

No corporate owners. Just people who care about this place.
