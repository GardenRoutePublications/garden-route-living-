About Us

Garden Route Living is an independent digital magazine dedicated to celebrating the people, places and lifestyle of South Africa's most beautiful coastline.

What We Cover

From the restaurateurs serving fresh linefish caught that morning, to the winemakers cultivating vines in the Harkerville hills. From the interior designers who understand our unique coastal climate, to the athletes who grew up running barefoot on our beaches and went on to win World Cups.

Our Approach

Every story in Garden Route Living is researched, written and photographed to magazine standards. We don't do clickbait. We don't chase algorithms. We create content that locals are proud to share and visitors find genuinely useful.

We work closely with businesses and organisations across the Garden Route, but our editorial remains independent. If we feature something, it's because we believe in it.
